# ZzzCam

A night camera and monitor app for parents. Turn one phone or tablet into a camera in your child's room, and another into a remote monitor. Video and audio stream directly between your devices — **nothing is uploaded to the cloud**.

## Features

- **Peer-to-peer WebRTC** — video and audio go directly between your devices
- **LAN or encrypted VPN (Tailscale)** — works at home or from anywhere without exposing your camera
- **Night-friendly light show** — soft animated colors help kids drift off
- **Ambient sounds** — real-time rain synthesis, no audio files to download
- **Sleep event detection** — optional local logging of movement and sounds
- **Ok-to-Wake timer** — a green light lets kids know when it's time to get up
